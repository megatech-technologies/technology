Our Programs

🐟

Aquaculture & Fish Farming

We specialize in catfish production and other aquaculture activities, delivering quality fish products through sustainable farming practices.

🌱

Crop Production & Farming

We cultivate vegetables, cassava, hibiscus (zobo), and other food and cash crops to support food security and agricultural growth.

🏭

Agro Processing & Value Addition

We transform farm produce into valuable products through processing, including smoked fish, dried products, moringa powder, and organic manure.

🌾

Agricultural Inputs Supply

We provide quality farm inputs including seeds, seedlings, fertilizers, animal nutrients, and other agricultural resources for improved productivity.

🍯

Agro Products Marketing & Distribution

We connect farmers and consumers by supplying and distributing agricultural commodities including honey, farm produce, and agro-based products.

🎓

Agricultural Training & Capacity Building

We empower individuals and communities through practical training in fish farming, crop production, agribusiness, and sustainable farming methods.

🌿

Farm Consultancy & Advisory Services

We provide expert guidance in farm setup, management, agribusiness planning, and technical agricultural support.

🔬

Demonstration Farms & Innovation Centers

We establish farms, hatcheries, nurseries, and processing centers for research, learning, improved farming techniques, and commercial production.

🤝

Agricultural Partnerships & Development

We collaborate with organizations, agencies, and stakeholders to promote sustainable agriculture and agribusiness development.
